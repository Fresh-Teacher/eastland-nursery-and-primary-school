Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some of the frequently asked questions and their respective answers

Does your school admit Muslim children only?

No! We admit pupils of all religions and different doctrines.

Do you teach following the national curriculum?

Yes! We teach following the guidelines provided by the Ministry of Education and Sports (MOES) together with the National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC)

Do you provide accomodation to boarding pupils?

Yes, we do! We have dormitories and well trained professional matrons and wardens.

Can your school develop my child's talent?

We participate in co-curricular activities which enable pupils to discover and develop their talents./p>

Do you give out bursaries at your school?

We have half and full bursaries for special categories of pupils like the those talented in Games and Sports and Music, Dance and Drama.
